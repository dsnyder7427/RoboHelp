<h2><a id="Required_Parameters_for_FILEWDL"></a>Required Parameters for 
 FILEWDL</h2>
<p class="Code">wd_list_ID</p>
<p>                Is 
 the list ID of a project</p>
<p class="Code">wd_SID</p>
<p><span style="color: #002060;">               </span> Is 
 the Worldox session ID</p>
<p class="Code">Html1</p>
<p><span style="color: #002060;">               </span> Should 
 be <span class="Code"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt;">/v4/errorlog/error.json</span></span>

 or not</p>

 FILEWDL</h2>
<p class="Code">wd_List_RecNum</p>

 it</p>
<p class="Code">wd_File_PathFile_Value</p>
<p>                Is
